Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?
You get full access to our Professional Suite (`150 active employees`, `Socket.IO sync`, `HR attendance`, and `dedicated MySQL DB pool`) for 14 days without requiring any credit card up front.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes! Because your data lives inside a standalone database schema, changing plans simply adjusts your employee seat limit (`25` → `150` → `Unlimited`) and activates advanced audit log tables instantly.
Is my database isolated with Encap.io / TaskFlow?
100% yes. Unlike shared-table competitors where all customers sit in one table with `WHERE tenant_id = X`, TaskFlow automatically provisions a separate database (`tenant_acme_corp`) at checkout.
Do you offer onboarding and support for enterprise teams?
Yes, our Enterprise tier includes a dedicated Solutions Architect to assist with migrating legacy Asana/Jira task histories, configuring custom RBAC role matrices, and setting up TOTP 2FA across your organization.
