Isolation Architecture

Why Database-Per-Tenant is the Only Acceptable Standard

In shared-table SaaS, a single missing WHERE tenant_id = X clause can expose your entire company's HR and financial data to a competitor. We eliminated this risk entirely.

  • AsyncLocalStorage Routing — Node.js transparently switches MySQL pools per request lifecycle without touching a single line of business logic.
  • Sub-Second Provisioning — All 21 tables + Super Admin seeded in under 800ms right at checkout completion.
  • LRU Pool Cleanup — TenantConnectionManager evicts idle connection pools after 30 minutes to keep production memory lean.
  • JWT Tenant Scoping — Tokens embed tenantDbName so every API call is cryptographically bound to the correct database.

FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

How fast is database provisioning after checkout?
Our automated TenantProvisioningService creates your dedicated database, executes all 21 system tables, and initializes your Super Admin account in under one second — right at checkout completion. No waiting, no setup emails.
Is my company's data physically separate from other companies?
Yes, completely. We don't co-mingle your records into a shared table. Every workspace gets its own MySQL database instance (e.g., tenant_your_company). A SQL query from Workspace A cannot ever access data from Workspace B — it's physically impossible by design.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription later?
Absolutely. Upgrading modifies your platform tier without any database migration — immediately unlocking advanced attendance, 2FA enforcement, and the custom RBAC builder. Downgrading takes effect at the next billing cycle.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
We retain your complete database backup for 90 days after cancellation. You can export a full MySQL dump at any time through the Super Admin panel. Your data is always yours — we never hold it hostage.
