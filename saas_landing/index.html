Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our multi-tenant isolation or subscription setup? We have answers.

How fast is the database provisioning after I complete checkout?
Our automated `TenantProvisioningService` creates your dedicated database, executes all 21 system tables (`users`, `projects`, `attendance`, `leaves`, etc.), and initializes your Super Admin credentials in less than 1 second right when you complete checkout.
Is my company data physically separated from other companies?
Yes! We do not pool your employee records into a single shared table. Every workspace has its own MySQL database instance (`tenant_yourcompany`). This means absolute cryptographic and physical isolation.
Can I upgrade from Starter to Professional later?
Absolutely. Upgrading instantly modifies your platform subscription tier without any database migration required, immediately unlocking advanced attendance, 2FA, and custom RBAC modules.
